# Swarming Media
- 1% (w/v) tryptone 
- 0.5% (w/v) yeast extract 
- 1% (w/v) NaCl 
- 0.6% (w/v) Eiken agar (not regular agar!) 
- 0.5% (w/v) Glucose (add from a sterile 20% glucose solution after autoclaving)

Recomendation:  
Make 1500mL: 15g tryp, 7.5g yeast, 15g NaCl, 9g agar in total (do not add to batch media)  
Partition into 12 bottles with 120mL[^ref-just] in each (0.75g agar each)

# Swimming Media
- 1% (w/v) tryptone 
- 0.5% (w/v) NaCl 
- 0.25% (w/v) agar 

Reccomendation:  
Make 1500 mL: 15g tryptone, 7.5g NaCl  
Partition into 120mL bottles with 0.3g agar each

# AAS Media
Same thing as swimming media but without the agar  
Need approximately 10 grams of cells; from experience a total of 3L has been enough, so make 1L batches in 2L E-flasks x 3.

# LB
- mixture in lab; see bottle for preparation instructions

[^ref-just]: 120 mLs chosen because it evenly paritions into agar plates (i.e. 30mLs of media x 4 plates)
